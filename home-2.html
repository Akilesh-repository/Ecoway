Transparent Workflow

From Decommissioning
to Certification.

We handle the entire chain of custody with rigorous tracking, ensuring zero data leaks and maximum environmental compliance.

1

Secure Logistics & Collection

Our vetted transport teams arrive at your facility. Assets are scanned, serialized, and palletized securely before loading into GPS-tracked vehicles, establishing an immediate chain of custody.

  • GPS Tracking Fleet
  • On-site Serial Scanning
2

Data Sanitization & Auditing

Upon facility intake, equipment is audited. All data-bearing devices undergo immediate NIST 800-88 compliant wiping. Failed drives are physically shredded to 2mm particles.

  • NIST 800-88 Wiping
  • DoD Physical Shredding
3

Processing & Material Recovery

Obsolete equipment is systematically dismantled. Precious metals, plastics, and glass are separated and routed to certified downstream refiners, ensuring a zero-landfill outcome.

  • R2v3 Certified Processing
  • Zero-Landfill Guarantee
