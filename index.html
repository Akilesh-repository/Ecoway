How It Works

The Secure Disposal Pipeline.

1. Secure Logistics & Pickup

Our GPS-tracked fleet collects assets directly from your facilities. A strict chain of custody is established the moment items leave your loading dock.

2. Data Destruction

All data-bearing devices undergo DoD 5220.22-M compliant wiping or physical shredding, guaranteeing complete data sanitization.

3. Recovery & Recycling

Viable hardware is refurbished for remarketing to maximize your ROI. End-of-life components are broken down to raw commodities.

4. Certification

Instantly download your Certificate of Destruction and Sustainability Impact Reports directly through the EcoWay Client Portal.
