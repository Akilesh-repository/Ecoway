Founded on Responsibility

Our journey began with a simple belief: technological advancement shouldn't cost the earth.

Who We Are

Redefining the lifecycle of corporate assets.

EcoWay was established to bridge the massive gap between rapid corporate technology upgrades and the severe environmental consequences of improper electronic waste disposal. We realized that enterprises needed a partner who could manage logistics, ensure absolute data destruction, and recycle responsibly—all under one roof.

Today, we are a global leader in IT Asset Disposition (ITAD). We process millions of pounds of e-waste annually, recovering precious commodities like gold, silver, and palladium, ensuring they are placed back into the manufacturing supply chain rather than polluting local ecosystems.

Absolute Security

Military-grade data destruction protocols ensuring zero data breaches.

Zero Landfill

A strict commitment to recycling and refurbishing 100% of received assets.

Our Purpose & Direction

The foundational pillars that guide our expansion, operations, and commitment to the environment.

Our Mission

To provide global enterprises with the most secure, transparent, and environmentally responsible IT Asset Disposition services available. We aim to mitigate corporate risk through absolute data destruction while extracting maximum value and minimizing the environmental footprint of retired technology.

  • Guarantee 100% data sanitization for all clients.
  • Divert hazardous materials entirely from public landfills.
  • Provide transparent, real-time ESG reporting.

Our Vision

To create a world where corporate technological progress does not equate to ecological degradation. We envision a fully circular economy where every obsolete server, laptop, and mobile device is viewed not as waste, but as the raw material for tomorrow’s innovations.

  • Establish processing hubs on every major continent.
  • Drive global policy changes regarding e-waste exports.
  • Innovate automated, AI-driven sorting and shredding.
Milestones

The EcoWay Journey

From a local recycling initiative to a global enterprise data security powerhouse.

2012

Foundation & First Facility

EcoWay was founded in California with a 10,000 sq ft facility focused on consumer electronics recycling. The primary goal was to prevent toxic heavy metals from entering local municipal landfills.

2015

Shift to Enterprise ITAD

Recognizing the massive gap in corporate data security, EcoWay pivoted to B2B IT Asset Disposition. We acquired our first industrial shredder and attained initial DoD data destruction compliance.

2019

R2v3 Certification Achieved

After rigorous auditing, EcoWay became one of the premier facilities to achieve the stringent R2v3 certification, solidifying our commitment to environmental health and worker safety.

  • Zero export of untreated hazardous waste
  • Full downstream vendor auditing
2022

Launch of the Client Portal

We introduced our proprietary, unified dashboard. Corporate clients gained the unprecedented ability to schedule pickups, track assets via GPS, and download automated Compliance Reports instantly.

2026

Global Expansion

Opening three new mega-facilities across Europe and Asia, bringing our secure enterprise ITAD and zero-landfill processing capabilities to Fortune 500 clients worldwide.
